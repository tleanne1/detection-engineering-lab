# Detection Validation Process

## Step 1 – Threat Research

Understand attacker behavior and ATT&CK techniques.

## Step 2 – Detection Design

Identify telemetry sources and create detection logic.

## Step 3 – Query Development

Develop KQL queries for telemetry collection.

## Step 4 – Validation

Execute detection against test or known malicious activity.

## Step 5 – False Positive Review

Identify and document benign triggers.

## Step 6 – Tuning

Adjust detection logic to improve fidelity.

## Step 7 – Deployment

Deploy to SIEM or EDR platform.

## Step 8 – Continuous Improvement

Regularly review and refine detections.
